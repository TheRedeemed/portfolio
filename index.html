Visit Shopping List

  • Users should be able to enter items they need to purchase
  • Users should be able edit items entered and may also be able to cancel their edit
  • Users should be able to check and uncheck items on the list
  • Users should also be able to permanently remove items from the list

Hot or Cold



Visit Hot or Cold

This is a Hot or Cold Number Guessing Game. The game goes like this:

  • The app generates a random number between 1 and 100 and keeps it hidden.
  • You need to guess until you can find the hidden secret number.
  • You will get feedback on how close :
    • ("hot") if your guess is greater than the generated number
    • ("cold") your guess is smaller than the generated number

Visit Hot or Cold
